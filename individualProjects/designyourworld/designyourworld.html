cad.

My process of creating my world. I used Autodesk Maya to 3d model it. I took a lot inspiration from older jazz clubs.

My world represents my love for jazz, specifically Brazilian bossa nova. I 3d modeled the exterior of a jazz club with the CAD program, Autodesk Maya. I was heavily inspired by the older design of American jazz clubs. I wanted to convey the classic "jazz aesthetic" with the darker atmosphere and purple brick. I find comfort in listening to bossa nova.
