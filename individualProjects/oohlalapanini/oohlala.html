Ooh La La Panini Website

Ooh La La Panini is a small, locally-owned restaurant in Santa Clarita, California. I collaborated with a friend to help a small, locally-owned business enhance their online presence and reach a wider audience.

Throughout the project, I was in charge of direct communication with the owners of the restaurant to gain a thorough understanding of their vision for the website.

Together, we created a clear branding online presence for Ooh La La Panini that accurately reflected the restaurant's unique atmosphere and menu offerings. The website was designed to be user-friendly and visually appealing, with clear and concise information about the restaurant's history, menu, and special events.
