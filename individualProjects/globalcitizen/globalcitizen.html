Global Citizen

Global Citizen is an interactive educational website aimed at educating teenagers about international subcultures. I collaborated with a friend on this project, and our goal was to create a shorter, module-like style of teaching that would make learning about different cultures engaging and accessible.

first plan.

Initally, we wanted to create a mobile app, heavily inspired by Duolingo. However, we realized that idea may not be the best way for teenagers to access education. Most of my peers are too lazy to download an app! So we needed to make a website.

topic brainstorming.

It was a big priority to cover holidays and aspects of less-celebrated cultures. We wanted our topics to showcase important things that EVERYONE should know.
